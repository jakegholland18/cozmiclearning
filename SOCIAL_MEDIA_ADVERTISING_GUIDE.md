## üìä HOW TO TRACK RESULTS

### In Facebook Ads Manager:

**Check Daily**:
1. Go to Ads Manager
2. Look at your campaign
3. Check these metrics:

**Key Metrics**:
- **Impressions**: How many people saw your ad
- **Clicks**: How many clicked to your website
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: Should be 1-3%
- **CPC (Cost Per Click)**: Should be under $1
- **Amount Spent**: Total spent so far

**Good Performance**:
- CTR: 2%+ (2 clicks per 100 views)
- CPC: Under $0.75
- Lots of clicks relative to spend

**Poor Performance**:
- CTR: Under 0.5%
- CPC: Over $2
- Very few clicks

**If Poor**: Change targeting, ad copy, or image

---

## üÜò TROUBLESHOOTING

### "My ad was rejected"

**Common Reasons**:
- Too much text in image (keep under 20% of image)
- Prohibited content (check Facebook ad policies)
- Misleading claims ("Cure" anything, "guaranteed" results)
- Landing page issues (broken link, doesn't match ad)

**Fix**: Edit ad, resubmit for review

### "I'm spending money but getting no clicks"

**Likely Issues**:
- Targeting too broad or too narrow
- Ad image not eye-catching
- Ad copy doesn't resonate
- CTA not clear

**Fix**:
- Try new image
- Test different headline
- Narrow targeting (add more specific interests)

### "I'm getting clicks but no signups"

**Likely Issues**:
- Signup page has issues (test it!)
- Stripe checkout confusing
- Wrong audience (not actually interested)
- Website loads slow

**Fix**:
- Test signup process yourself
- Ask friend to try signing up
- Check website speed
- Make sure Stripe test mode shows clearly
