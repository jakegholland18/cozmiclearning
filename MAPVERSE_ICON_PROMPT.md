# MapVerse Icon - AI Image Generation Prompt

## Subject Information
- **Name**: MapVerse
- **Category**: Geography (Core Subject)
- **Description**: Explore world geography, cultures, regions, and spatial understanding
- **Theme**: Maps, exploration, world discovery, navigation

## Design Requirements
- **Format**: Circular planet/sphere icon (consistent with other CozmicLearning planets)
- **Style**: Vibrant, cosmic, playful, educational
- **Color Palette**: Earth tones mixed with cosmic colors (blues, greens, teals, purples)
- **Target Audience**: K-12 students (grades 1-12)
- **File Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Recommended Size**: 512x512px minimum

## Image Generation Prompt (Copy & Paste into AI Image Generator)

```
A vibrant, cosmic-themed circular planet icon for "MapVerse" geography learning app. The planet should feature:

MAIN ELEMENTS:
- A spherical planet that looks like a stylized globe/world map
- Visible continents and oceans with bright, saturated colors
- Cartographic elements like compass roses, latitude/longitude grid lines, or navigation symbols integrated into the surface
- Unfolded map sections floating around the planet like cosmic ribbons
- Small location pins or waypoint markers glowing on the surface

COLOR SCHEME:
- Deep ocean blues (#0066CC, #00A8E8)
- Vibrant land greens (#32CD32, #00FF7F)
- Warm earth browns (#8B4513, #CD853F)
- Cosmic purple/pink accents (#9D4EDD, #FF006E) for the atmosphere
- Glowing teal highlights (#00F2FF) for navigation elements
- Space background with subtle star field

ATMOSPHERE & EFFECTS:
- Swirling cosmic atmosphere around the planet
- Glow effect emanating from the planet
- Small sparkles or stars surrounding it
- Map grid lines that glow softly
- Vintage compass rose integrated somewhere in the design
- Navigation routes or dotted pathways wrapping around the sphere

STYLE:
- Playful and inviting, not overly realistic
- Pixar/animated film quality
- Educational but fun
- Clean, modern icon design
- Suitable for children and teens
- Cosmic/space theme consistent with other learning "planets"
- Slightly 3D with depth and dimension

The overall vibe should be: "Adventure, exploration, discovering our amazing world, navigation through knowledge"
```

## Post-Processing
After generation:
1. Remove background (make transparent)
2. Resize to 512x512px
3. Optimize for web (compress PNG)
4. Save as `map_verse.png`
5. Place in `/website/static/` directory
