## üéØ KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### 1. **Tight Basic/Essentials Plans**
- Just enough to be useful (10 questions/day for students)
- Creates natural friction for power users
- Drives upgrades to premium/complete

### 2. **Generous Premium/Complete Plans**
- 5-10√ó multipliers make the value obvious
- Users feel "unlimited" even with limits
- Premium feels like an incredible deal for just $5 more

### 3. **Per-Student Accounting for Parent/Homeschool**
- Parent/Homeschool plans include actual student accounts
- Limits are per student for questions/practice
- PowerGrid is shared (family planning together)

### 4. **Teacher Plans Are Tools Only**
- No student usage included
- Focuses on lesson planning, assignment creation, analytics
- Students must have their own accounts (free or paid)

### 5. **Cost Sustainability**
- Even "generous" premium plans maintain 93%+ margins
- Realistic 30% usage keeps API costs low
- Room for power users without breaking budget
