## üé® WHY THIS PRICING WORKS

### 1. **Clear Value Ladder**
- Free: "Try it out" (3 subjects, limited)
- Basic: "Everything I need" ($9.99 = affordable)
- Premium: "Serious learning" ($14.99 = still reasonable)
- Just $5 difference between tiers (easy upgrade decision)

### 2. **Daily Limits Feel Generous**
- Basic: 50 questions/day = 1,500/month (most use 15/day)
- Premium: 200 questions/day = 6,000/month (most use 60/day)
- **Users rarely hit limits but feel unrestricted**

### 3. **Competitive Positioning**
- **vs IXL ($19.95/subject):** You're $9.99 for ALL subjects
- **vs Khanmigo ($4/mo):** You offer full curriculum, not just chat
- **vs Tutoring ($40-100/hr):** $9.99/month = unlimited tutoring

### 4. **Psychological Pricing**
- $9.99 not $10 (sub-$10 feels affordable)
- $14.99 not $15 (premium but reasonable)
- $19.99, $34.99, $24.99, $59.99 (all .99 endings)
