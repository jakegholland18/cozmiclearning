## ğŸ¨ WHY THIS PRICING WORKS

### 1. **Psychological Pricing**
- $4.99, not $5.00 (feels cheaper)
- $9.99, not $10.00 (standard sweet spot)
- $19.99, not $20.00 (value tier)
- "Most Popular" badge on middle tier (anchoring)

### 2. **Daily vs Monthly Limits**
- **Daily limits feel more generous** than hourly
- 100 questions/day = 3000/month (students rarely use that much)
- Prevents burst abuse while allowing flexibility

### 3. **Tiered Feature Access**
- Free: Enough to try (3 subjects, 20 questions/day)
- Basic: Everything a student needs ($4.99 = Starbucks latte)
- Premium: Power users, serious learners ($9.99 = Netflix)
- Complete: Families, unlimited feel ($19.99 = cheaper than tutoring)

### 4. **Market Positioning**
**Competitors:**

- Khanmigo: $4/month (ChatGPT interface only)

- **At competitive or LOWER prices**
