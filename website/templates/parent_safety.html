About Content Moderation

CozmicLearning uses advanced AI technology to monitor all student interactions and ensure appropriate, educational content. Our system checks for:

  • Inappropriate language - Profanity and offensive content
  • Academic dishonesty - Attempts to cheat or have AI do homework
  • Safety concerns - Content that violates OpenAI's usage policies
  • Off-topic abuse - Attempts to misuse the educational AI

When content is flagged, it's automatically blocked and logged for review. High-risk content triggers immediate parent notifications.
