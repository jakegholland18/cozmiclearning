Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now }}

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: We collect name, email address, password (stored securely as a hash), and date of birth for all users.

Student Learning Data: We collect questions asked, AI-generated responses, assessment results, scores, time spent learning, subject and topic selections, character preferences, and progress tracking data.

Parent/Teacher Data: For parents and teachers, we collect name, email, and account linking information.

Subscription Details: Plan type, billing frequency, trial dates, and subscription status.

Session Data: Login times, session duration, and activity logs.

2. Use of Information

We use collected data to:

  • Personalize learning experiences and adapt difficulty levels
  • Track student progress and generate reports for parents and teachers
  • Manage subscriptions and process payments
  • Improve our AI tutoring algorithms and educational content
  • Monitor for inappropriate content and ensure child safety
  • Comply with legal requirements and COPPA regulations

3. Cookies and Session Management

We use secure session cookies to maintain user login state, track XP and tokens, store character preferences, and manage authentication. Cookies are HTTPOnly and Secure to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Payment Information

All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe, a PCI DSS compliant payment processor. We do not store credit card numbers, CVV codes, or other sensitive payment information on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: CozmicLearning is subject to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Age Verification: We require all users to provide their date of birth during signup.

Parental Consent for Children Under 13: Children under 13 years of age are required to sign up using a parent access code. This ensures verifiable parental consent before we collect any personal information from children under 13.

Data We Collect from Children: For children under 13 with parental consent, we collect:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Learning questions and AI-generated educational responses
  • Progress data, scores, and time spent learning
  • Character and subject preferences

Content Moderation: All questions asked by students are automatically moderated for inappropriate content using AI filtering and keyword detection. Parents are notified of any flagged content.

Parental Rights: Parents have the right to:

  • Review all personal information collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Revoke consent and refuse further collection of their child's data
  • View all questions their child has asked and responses received

To exercise these rights, parents should contact us at jakegholland18@gmail.com.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share student personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We share limited data with:

  • OpenAI: Student questions are sent to OpenAI's API for generating educational responses. Questions are moderated before sending.
  • Stripe: For payment processing (parents/teachers only, never student payment data).
  • Email Service Provider: For sending notifications and reports to parents and teachers.

7. Data Retention

We retain student learning data for as long as the account remains active. When an account is deleted, all personal information is permanently removed within 30 days, except as required by law.

8. Data Protection and Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Password hashing using industry-standard bcrypt
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • HTTPOnly and Secure session cookies to prevent XSS attacks
  • Input validation and sanitization to prevent SQL injection
  • Content moderation to filter inappropriate content
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

9. Your Rights

All users (or parents on behalf of children under 13) have the right to:

  • Access their personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their account and data
  • Export their learning data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, COPPA inquiries, or to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:
