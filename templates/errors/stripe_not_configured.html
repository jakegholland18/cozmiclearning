How to Fix This

1 Create Product in Stripe Dashboard

Go to Stripe Dashboard ‚Üí Products

  • Click "Add product"
  • Name: "{{ role|capitalize }} {{ plan|capitalize }} Plan"
  • Description: "{{ plan|capitalize }} subscription for {{ role }} users"
2 Add a Price

Under the product you just created:

  • Click "Add another price"
  • Pricing model: Standard pricing
  • Set your price amount (e.g., $9.99)
  • Billing period: {{ billing|capitalize }}
  • Click "Save"
3 Copy the Price ID

After creating the price, you'll see a Price ID starting with price_...

Copy this ID - you'll need it for the next step.

4 Add to Render Environment Variables

Go to your Render dashboard:

  • Navigate to your service ‚Üí Environment
  • Click "Add Environment Variable"
  • Key: {{ missing_key }}
  • Value: price_xxxxxxxxxxxxx (paste the Price ID you copied)
  • Click "Save Changes"
5 Redeploy Your App

Render will automatically redeploy when you save environment variables. Wait for the deployment to complete, then test the payment flow again.
