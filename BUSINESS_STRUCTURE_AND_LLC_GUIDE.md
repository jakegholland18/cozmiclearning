## üìã STEP-BY-STEP: HOW TO FILE LLC (DIY)

### **Method 1: Your State's Website** (Cheapest)

**Step 1: Choose Business Name** (5 min)
- Name must include "LLC"
- Examples: "CozmicLearning, LLC" or "Cozmic Learning LLC"
- Check availability: YourState.gov/business-search

**Step 2: Choose Registered Agent** (2 min)
- Can be YOU (your home address) = $0
- Or pay service ($100-300/year)
- Receives legal documents on behalf of LLC

**Step 3: File Articles of Organization** (30 min)
- Go to your state's Secretary of State website
- Find "File LLC" or "Register Business"
- Fill out online form:
  - LLC name
  - Your name (member/owner)
  - Registered agent address
  - Business purpose (e.g., "Educational technology services")
- Pay filing fee ($50-200)

**Step 4: Get EIN from IRS** (5 min)
- Go to: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- Fill out form
- Get EIN instantly (free)
- You need this for:
  - Bank account
  - Taxes
  - Stripe/payment processing

**Step 5: Create Operating Agreement** (30 min)
- Download free template online
- Fill in:
  - LLC name
  - Your name (sole member)
  - Ownership: 100%
  - Management: Member-managed
- Sign and date it
- Keep in records (usually not filed with state)

**Step 6: Wait for Approval** (1-2 weeks)
- State reviews your filing
- Emails/mails certificate of formation
- You're now official!

**Total Time**: 1-2 hours of work, 1-2 weeks waiting
